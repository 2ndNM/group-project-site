About the Project

This project explores transportation challenges and solutions across various landscapes, blending engineering and innovation.

This expanded section explains how we conducted fieldwork, collected data, and applied transportation engineering principles in real-world scenarios. Collaboration, sustainability, and innovation were the key driving forces behind our project outcomes.
